Sentient: A Website for Sentiment Analysis of Tagalog-English Feedbacks using Logistic Regression

Sentiment analysis (also known as opinion mining or emotion AI) is the use of natural language processing, text analysis, computational linguistics, and biometrics to systematically identify, extract, quantify, and study affective states and subjective information

ABOUT Sentient

Sentiment analysis is a process of analyzing and categorizing opinions expressed in text data to determine the sentiment behind them. It involves using natural language processing and machine learning techniques to extract and classify subjective information, such as positive, negative, or neutral emotions.


The system will also include a live graph that shows the count of positive and negative feedback. This graph will update in real-time as new data is processed by the system. Additionally, the system will have the ability to delete or add items as needed to ensure that the data being analyzed is relevant and up-to-date.

PROJECT SCOPE

The aim of this project is to develop a sentiment analysis model that can classify Tagalog text into positive and negative sentiments. Sentiment analysis is a powerful tool for understanding public opinion, customer feedback, and brand reputation.
Determine the sentiment of Tagalog-speaking audiences towards a particular brand or company.
Provide real-time feedback to Tagalog-speaking customers by analyzing their sentiments.
Identify potential risks or crises by analyzing negative sentiment in Tagalog-speaking communities
Use the insights gained from the sentiment analysis to improve customer experience and make informed decisions.
